📖 Flags System

Each day you are expected to do your chore and clean your room.

  • When you forget, you get a point.
  • When you reach 3 flags, you lose your phone/device.
  • To get your device back, you must do extra chores to get back to zero.
  • 3 flags = 3 EXTRA chores before your device comes back.
  • If more days go by without doing chores, more flags pile up — more extra chores to work off!

Do your chores every day and you keep your device. Simple! ✅
