📖 Points System Rules

Each day you are expected to do your chore and clean your room.

  • When you forget, you get a point.
  • When you reach 3 points, you lose your phone/device.
  • To get your device back, you must do extra chores to get back to zero points.
  • That means at 3 points = 3 EXTRA chores before you get your device back.
  • If days keep going by without doing chores, points keep adding up — meaning even more extra chores to work off!

Bottom line: Do your chores every day and you'll never lose your device. Simple! ✅
